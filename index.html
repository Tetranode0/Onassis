About Me

It may be more than coincidence that straying from the buildings of Van Alen, or the tech design of Dieter Rams coincided with a decline in spirit, and aesthetics. It is consequential that we label collective disillusion of reaching, 'developed nation' status. Calling a nation developed is a pejorative expression that mocks a loss in will of its' people. To borrow philosophy from Steve Jobs, the aesthetic element mediates our engagement with the world. Outright removal, or desecration, ruins the human experience. Culture may raise the individual, yet it is the individual is where culture is born. I posit innovation and beauty are a weapon and shield. To describe my life's work in brief: reforming the 'developed' worldview of 'progress'.
